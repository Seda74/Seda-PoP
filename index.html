Bowl (Crew Training and Leveling)

Crew

New crew members require training before mainline service. Utilize your three ships strategically.

Use Port Focus settings to gather resources and train crew on safer voyages.

A voyage to the Scythe with an experience-boosting unit can raise a level 0 unit to level 2, and a second voyage to level 3.

With the new office, you can expand your crew setup from three merchants to four, and ensure to bring a merchant on every applicable mission.

Obtain three to four Judges of Dice (JOD), as these will be used until the final (Shield) region.

Loop (Distance Focus)

Notes

The Loop is the longest region to unlock the next upgrade, and has low resource rewards per mission. Focus on rushing distance while protecting your crew with lifeboats.

Terracotta Merchants offer a small improvement and are a low priority buy. Purchase them near the end of the region for some leveling before The Shield.

Shield (Endgame Focus)

Notes

Shield is the final region. While there's no further region to unlock, some upgrades require reaching the maximum distance of 6,000,000.

After acquiring all Shield buyables, consider returning to Scythe and Bowl for resources to manipulate rewards with totems.

Optimal Endgame Crew

Optimal endgame captains are 2 Morale, 1 Combat, and 2 Seafaring captains (each with 4 appropriate +1% traits).

For maximum resources and trade goods: 6 MCS, 4 TCM, 3 Exiles

For a more balanced approach, adjust crew based on your playstyle and needs.
